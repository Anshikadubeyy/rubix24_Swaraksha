FAQs

What kind of Ride you can book from YatriSeva

You can book following kinds of rides from YatriSeva: airport and intercity / outstation . You can book outstation for both one-way transfers & round-trip transfers.

How can I book online?

YatriSeva helps you with online booking at the best prices, comparatively much lower than the local vendors. Also you can use promo codes and offers on the CarRide website & app on online cab booking to get extra discounts.

What are the advantages of online booking?

Online booking not only helps you with best prices but also helps you with the convenience of paying through multiple payment options (like Debit Card, Credit Card, eWallets etc.). You can easily compare prices and choose various categories of cabs like Hatchback cars, Sedan and SUV.

What are the popular outstation Ride booking routes?

Some of the popular outstation booking routes are Pune to Mumbai , Delhi to Jaipur , Delhi to Agra , Pondicherry to Chennai cabs, Ahmedabad to Vadodara , Bangalore to Mysore cabs and Mumbai to Pune .
