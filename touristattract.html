Balaji Mandir

Description:

The Nerul Balaji Temple is an almost-exact replica of the Tirupati Balaji Temple, and also replicates certain aspects of the Venkateswara temple at Tirupati. The primary deity worshipped in this temple is Shri Balaji, which is a name ascribed to a form of Lord Hanuman is different parts of our country.
